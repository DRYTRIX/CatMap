Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Summary

CatMap is designed to be anonymous. You do not create an account or provide personal information to use the map.

What we store

  • Device token — a random ID generated in your browser's localStorage. It is sent as X-Device-Token so you can confirm sightings once, report content, and delete your own posts. It is not linked to your identity.
  • Sightings you submit — photo (EXIF metadata is stripped before storage), optional description, and map coordinates.
  • Anonymous analytics (optional) — if you accept the consent banner, Google Analytics 4 may collect aggregated usage data (page views, feature interactions). IP addresses are anonymized.

What we do not collect

  • Names, email addresses, or login credentials
  • Precise device identifiers beyond the anonymous token above
  • Photo EXIF after upload (GPS and camera metadata are removed)

Moderation

Users can report sightings. Content may be hidden automatically after multiple reports. Uploaded photos are checked for cat-like content before being published.

Third parties

  • OpenStreetMap — map tiles and place search (Nominatim)
  • Google Analytics — only when you opt in via the consent banner. Google Privacy Policy

Contact

Questions or concerns: open a GitHub issue or visit drytrix.com.
